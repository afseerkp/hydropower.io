Fluid System and Pipeline Division

Executing projects involving all types and pipe sizes. Carbon Steel, Ductile Iron, GRP & GRE, RC, UPVC & HDPE pipe materials of diameter sizes ranging from 100mm (4 inches) to 2000mm (80 inches). Handling low pressure and high-pressure systems (6 bar to 120 bar). The main activities of this division are;

  • Water Transmission, Pipelines & Pumping Stations;
  • Water Distribution Networks & Pumping Stations;
  • Sewage, Drainage & Stormwater Networks & Pumping Stations;
  • Seawater Intake Stations;
  • RO Plants;
  • Mechanical Piping;
  • Oil & Gas Lifting & Booster Stations;
  • Sewage Treatment Plants;
  • Process & Power Plants;
  • Flowlines;
  • Metering;
  • Electrical & Instrumentation;
  • SCADA & Industrial Automation.

Environmental

HYDROPOWER aims to have a sustainability performance it can be proud of. It aims to contribute to sustainable development and earn the confidence of customers, joint venture partners and the society at large. To do so, HYDROPOWER has established the following statement of commitment, HSE Policy and HSE objectives, as shown below.

Statement of Commitment HYDROPOWER and its Group Companies are committed to:

  • Pursue the goal of no harm to people and the community
  • Protect the environment and biodiversity

Be transparent in the public reporting of the Groups HSE performance Sustainability in HYDROPOWER is being built from the solid foundation of our long standing HSE Policy and commitment to an outstanding HSE performance. HYDROPOWERs HSE Policy drives much of our operations and we aim to perform as well as, if not better than, our international joint venture business partners.
